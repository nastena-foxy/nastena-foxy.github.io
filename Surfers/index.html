DESCRIPTION

Easy to ride, care free surfing craft that’s fun for everyone, paddles well, super fast down the line and great for any level of surfing depending on who rides it.

The Stoker has elements of Stokesy’s original pro model with new tweaks, enabling a progression to what Stokesy wants to achieve when he gets in the water for a freesurf. He wants to blow up and have some fun. We’ve added more drive to the Stoker with a slightly more parallel rail and pulled the tail shape in slightly to get more hold on a bottom turn, dropped the rail shape down a touch to increase rail hold through bigger turns and lengthened the board an inch whilst raising the nose rocker to enable the board to hold in on tight pockets and waves with a lot of shape.Most importantly this is a ‘do everything’ board. Just get it out there and have some fun.

FEATURES

  • Tail Nice average width squash tail for a balance between hold and release
  • Template Performance shortboard with slightly more foam and a slightly wider tail for use in a wider range of conditions.
  • Rider ability: Intermediate to Pro
  • Fin system: FCSII (Not included)
  • Traditional polyester construction 2 x 4 Oz glass on the deck, 1 x 4oz glass on the bottom
